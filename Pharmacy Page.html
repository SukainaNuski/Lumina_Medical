Order Medicines Online

We now offer the convenience of ordering your medicines online. Simply fill out the form below or follow our step-by-step guide to place an order.

Steps to Order Medicines Online

Step 1: Register

Sign up for an account to easily manage your orders and prescriptions.

Step 2: Upload Prescription

Upload a valid prescription from your doctor to order prescription medicines.

Step 3: Add Medicines

Select the medicines you need and add them to your cart for checkout.

Step 4: Payment & Delivery

Pay securely online and have your medicines delivered to your doorstep.
