Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers comprehensive care for heart conditions, including diagnostic testing, treatment, and management of heart diseases. Our team of experts uses advanced technology to provide personalized care.

Neurology

We provide advanced care for neurological disorders, including stroke, epilepsy, and chronic headaches, with a focus on accurate diagnosis and effective treatment. Our specialists are dedicated to improving your neurological health.

Paediatrics

Our paediatric services include routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for various childhood illnesses to ensure the healthy development of your child. We offer a friendly and caring environment for children.

Orthopaedics

We offer expert care for musculoskeletal conditions, including fractures, arthritis, and joint replacements, aiming to improve mobility and quality of life. Our orthopaedic surgeons are skilled in both surgical and non-surgical treatments.

Gynaecology

Our gynaecology services cover routine exams, prenatal care, and treatment for various reproductive health issues, ensuring comprehensive women's health care. We prioritize comfort and privacy in all our services.

Ophthalmology

We provide specialized care for eye conditions, including vision correction, cataract surgery, and treatment for various eye diseases to maintain optimal eye health. Our ophthalmologists use the latest technology for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
